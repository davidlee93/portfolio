About Me

I like to build things – usually with technology, and a side of grit. I've always been fascinated with how things are built, understanding how they work and getting into the specifics in order to build them better.

I write simple and coherent code, building full stack JavaScript applications with experience in React/Redux, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB to name a few.

In a previous career I worked in a biotechnology company as a biosample lab associate.

But it was my passion for building and development that changed my career path. Having an idea move from thought to reality on a page and the addicting sense of satisfaction that comes with solving a problem is what I love about coding.

When I'm not writing code, you'll probably find me enjoying food, a book, or some exercise. I love exploring new places, every sport with a ball, the beach, hiking, and the great outdoors. I'm passionate about people, learning and talking about life and (of course) food!
